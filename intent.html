pTA

Parent Teacher Association The Parent Teacher Association plays an active and important role, appointing sub-committees to oversee the school bus system, after-school coaching in games and the canteen service. The PTA Management Committee comprises parent representatives from each standard as well as teachers. The committee meets once a month. PTA membership is open to all parents of students currently enrolled, on payment of an annual membership fee of Rs 50 only.
